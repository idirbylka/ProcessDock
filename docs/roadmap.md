# Roadmap

## Overview

ProcessDock is developed incrementally, with a focus on establishing a solid and extensible foundation before expanding into more advanced capabilities.

The roadmap reflects a progression from core execution tracking toward broader support for business workflows.

---

## Current State

The system currently provides:

* Project management (top-level structure)
* WorkItem management (units of work)
* WorkSession tracking (execution and time measurement)
* Introduction of a service layer for business logic
* Layered architecture with clear separation of concerns

---

## Short-Term Focus

The next stage focuses on strengthening the existing foundation:

* Authentication and authorization
* Improved validation and error handling
* WorkItem lifecycle management
* Consistency improvements across API endpoints

---

## Mid-Term Direction

Once the foundation is stable, the system will expand its ability to represent and analyze operational data:

* Aggregation of execution metrics (e.g. total duration per WorkItem and Project)
* Reporting capabilities
* Cost and performance analysis
* Refinement of domain rules and constraints

---

## Long-Term Direction

The system is designed to evolve into a modular platform capable of supporting broader operational workflows.

Potential areas of expansion include:

* Multi-user collaboration
* Flexible workflow coordination
* Notification mechanisms
* Approval processes
* Integration with external systems

---

## Design Approach

The roadmap is intentionally iterative:

* Features are introduced when supported by a stable domain model
* Complexity is added progressively and where it provides clear value
* The architecture is continuously refined to support future growth

---

## Vision

ProcessDock explores how a modular backend system can support multiple operational needs within a unified architecture, while remaining adaptable to evolving requirements.
